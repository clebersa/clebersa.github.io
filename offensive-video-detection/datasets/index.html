Offensive Video Detection

Datasets

This page contains the OffVidPT dataset, a dataset of offensive videos used for a classification task of offensive video detection. The dataset has 400 videos and it is divided in 5 feature sets: descriptions, tags, titles, transcriptions and statistics. All files are in the CSV format.
